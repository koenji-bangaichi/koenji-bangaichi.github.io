NO LIMIT TOKYO: KOENJI BANGAICHI*

Date: 9/22 - 10/1, 2023
Location: Koenji neighborhood + other areas in Tokyo

This year, Koenji community will host a major global cultural exchange festivity that recklessly invites people from the world's underground cultures, mainly from the Asian region. Over the course of 10 days, a plethora of small events spanning music, art, and social movements will emerge, including live performances, DJ events, exhibitions, documentary screenings, talk shows, theater plays, and social gatherings. There will be a plenty of gatherings to make stateless human connections - come join us and be festive together!

*BANGAICHI refers to a place that exists outside the designated order on a map, representing a location that exists beyond the established boundaries or rules.

Use #koenji_bangaichi to share the happenings around you to make your gatherings even more chaotic!
