NO LIMIT TOKYO


NO LIMIT TOKYO 高円寺番外地

Date: September 22nd - October 1st, 2023
Location: Various locations near Koenji

An event of global exchange that irresponsibly invites people from the world's underground cultures, with a focus on the Asian region, to Koenji! During the 10-day period, numerous small events ranging from live performances, DJ events, exhibitions, documentary screenings, talk shows, theater, and drinking parties, covering music, art, and social issues!
